Who we are

One vision. Two languages.

Start a project

English

Born in Ukraine. Building without borders.

Our story began in Ukraine, where for 18 years we have worked at the intersection of film production, scenic construction and custom fabrication. We learned our craft on demanding sets — turning ambitious creative ideas into physical environments made for the camera and built to perform.

When the war divided our team, it changed our geography, but not our purpose. A new branch was established in Toronto while production continued in Ukraine. Today, CAD Art Design works across Canada, Ukraine and Europe, bringing the same experience, resilience and attention to detail to every project.

Français

Notre histoire a commencé en Ukraine, où nous travaillons depuis 18 ans dans la production cinématographique, la construction de décors et la fabrication sur mesure.
